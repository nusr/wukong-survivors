# Game Audio Files

This directory contains all the audio files used in the game. The audio files are organized into two categories:

## Sound Effects (sfx/)
These are short audio clips used for various game events:

1. `player_hit.mp3` - Player being hit sound
2. `player_death.mp3` - Player death sound
3. `level_up.mp3` - Player level up sound
4. `weapon_hit.mp3` - Weapon hitting enemy sound
5. `enemy_hit.mp3` - Enemy being hit sound
6. `enemy_death.mp3` - Enemy death sound
7. `critical_hit.mp3` - Critical hit sound
8. `exp_pickup.mp3` - Experience pickup sound
9. `gold_pickup.mp3` - Gold pickup sound
10. `powerup.mp3` - Powerup pickup sound
11. `ui_click.mp3` - UI button click sound
12. `ui_hover.mp3` - UI button hover sound
13. `ui_select.mp3` - UI selection sound
14. `reward_appear.mp3` - Reward appearing sound
15. `wave_start.mp3` - Wave start warning sound
16. `victory.mp3` - Victory sound effect
17. `warning.mp3` - Warning/alert sound

## Music Tracks (music/)
These are longer audio tracks used for background music:

1. `menu.mp3` - Main menu background music
2. `game_theme.mp3` - Gameplay background music
3. `boss_theme.mp3` - Boss battle background music
4. `victory.mp3` - Victory celebration music

## Audio Style Guidelines

Please refer to "Black Myth: Wukong" for audio style reference:
- Sound effects should be impactful and immersive
- Music should be epic and atmospheric, with traditional Chinese elements
- Maintain consistent volume levels across all audio files
- Use high-quality mp3 format (128kbps or higher)

## How to Add Audio Files

1. Download or create the audio files following the naming convention above
2. Place them in the appropriate directory (`sfx/` for sound effects, `music/` for music)
3. Ensure the files are in mp3 format
